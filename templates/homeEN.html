Welcome to Linha de Apoio ao Caloiro

We know how hard it can be to get started in university.

The Linha de Apoio ao Caloiro is here to help you.

Our Mission

Give an introduction to the Freshmen of Ubian society and Covilhã. Facilitator for freshmen/new students to integrate into the system of classes, rooms, departments and even transport (both within Covilhã and external, buses and trains for example) including a list of taxi and bus numbers in order to facilitate your adaptation in a new city. Stimulate community interaction through dynamic competitions.

Vision

Create a platform for all Ubians, who through the community, have a form of support, motivation, competition, unity and integration with freshmen and other members of the Ubian community.

Our Values

  • Quality: We provide accurate information to help freshmen integrate into UBI and Covilhã, focusing on usability and service excellence.
  • Transparency: We prioritize clarity and accessibility, with easy-to-understand maps and competitions for all users.
  • Sustainability: We offer maps, resources for freshmen, and competitions, with constant updates and new features to meet students' needs.
  • Innovation: We promote digitization, local partnerships, and sustainable practices, fostering community interaction and project growth.

Our Services

Our services are designed to support you from the start of your university journey. We are here to answer all your questions and ensure you have a great start in the academic world.

  • Geografic Map: Help you find your way around Covilhã (Google Maps).
  • Skedule: Access to your schedule and information about the university.
  • Canteen Information: Access to the information of the canteen (prices, menu, etc.).
